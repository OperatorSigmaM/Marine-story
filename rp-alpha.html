1. Device Purpose

The RP-α device is designed for harmonic interaction with matter through precise resonance frequency tuning with minimal energy load. The primary goal is to create a practical mechanism for subtle interaction with substances through acoustic, mechanical, and field waves.

Application Areas:
  • Research of material resonance properties
  • Substance structure modification
  • Bioacoustic experiments
  • Precision object levitation

2. Operating Principle

The device operates on the principle of adaptive resonance interaction with matter:

  1. Scanning: the device scans objects (e.g., stone or water) using spectral sensors and microphones/vibration detectors.
  2. Recognition: the built-in algorithm identifies material resonance peaks.
  3. Tuning: the microcontroller adjusts output frequencies of wave sources (piezoelements, quartz resonators).
  4. Impact: resonance waves are amplified inside the resonance chamber and directed at the object with high precision.
  5. Monitoring: the system monitors material response and corrects the impact in real-time.
  6. Final phase: the object's structure either changes (softening, structuring) or activates (droplet levitation, plant growth enhancement).
ATTENTION:
The device operates only in harmonic interaction mode. Forceful impact is architecturally excluded.
